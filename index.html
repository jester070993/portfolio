About me

I obtained a BA in Sociology from University of California, Santa Cruz. While I absolutely love helping people, after working in the realm of social work I discovered it really wasn't my true passion. I ended up attending and completing a coding bootcamp, and a whole new world of posibilities opened up.

UX/UI design as been a keen interest with me ever since. How a person interacts with a visual interface, why they tend to gravitate towards certain aspect, and how to use that knowledge in order to create a better user interaction between human and computer has really struck an interest in me. I love designing, creating and building, especially if I have the general idea or basis for a given design, that is when I am really in my element.
